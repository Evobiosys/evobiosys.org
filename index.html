About

What is EvoBioSys?

EvoBioSys — Evolving Living Systems — is a framework and growing network for people and projects that take living systems seriously. Not as metaphor, but as method.

The name encodes three nested dimensions: Evo (evolutionary growth and development), Bio (living processes and stewardship), and Sys (systemic technology and infrastructure). Together, they form a holarchy — each complete in itself, each part of something larger.

We organize through nested collectives, each with its own purpose and autonomy, connected through shared principles and mutual support. Current initiatives span community building, financial stewardship, open-source tooling, educational development, and publishing.

Founded and steered by Jakob Possert, EvoBioSys is rooted in Vienna and oriented toward European sovereignty, developmental depth, and practical idealism.
