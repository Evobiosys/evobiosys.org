Article I — Purpose

What Is Gruber Works

Gruber Works is the brand name for a legal entity designed to serve as the institutional backbone of the EvoBioSys network. Where the holons are living, evolving projects — software, communities, creative ventures — Gruber Works provides the formal structure that allows them to interface with the world of contracts, taxation, banking, and regulatory compliance.

The need arose from a simple reality: as the EvoBioSys ecosystem grew, its projects began requiring things that only a legal entity can provide. Invoice issuance. VAT registration. Contractual capacity. Liability boundaries. Rather than spinning up ad-hoc solutions for each venture, Gruber Works was conceived as a single, sovereign legal home — a holding structure with the flexibility to house any holon that needs one.

The Founding Principle

A creation should not need to compromise its nature in order to exist legally. Gruber Works exists so that each holon can meet institutional requirements on its own terms — professionalizing with heart, not by abandoning it.

The entity is connected to idea2.Life for sovereign infrastructure, and to WonderFul Consulting — Jakob's existing consulting entity — which will fold into or operate alongside the SE as the structure matures. Gruber Works is not a startup. It is a legal vessel designed for longevity: a container for decades of work, not a vehicle for a single exit.

★ Societas Europae ★
Article II — Legal Form

Societas Europae — The European Company

The SE (Societas Europae, Latin for "European Company") is a legal form established by EU Council Regulation No. 2157/2001. It creates a company type that is genuinely European rather than merely national — a single legal entity recognized across all EU and EEA member states.

An SE can transfer its registered office from one member state to another without dissolving and re-incorporating. It operates under a unified set of EU-level rules supplemented by the national law of its state of registration. This gives it a portability that no national company form can match: if regulatory or strategic conditions shift, the entity moves with you.

I

Cross-Border Mobility

Transfer the registered office to any EU/EEA state without liquidation. The company persists as the same legal person.

II

EU-Wide Recognition

A single legal form understood by every member state. No need for subsidiary structures to operate across borders.

III

Governance Flexibility

Choose between a one-tier (board of directors) or two-tier (management + supervisory board) governance model.

IV

Institutional Credibility

The SE suffix signals serious European commitment. It carries weight with partners, regulators, and financial institutions.

For Gruber Works, the SE form is a deliberate choice. It aligns with the EvoBioSys philosophy of sovereignty — EU data protection, EU corporate law, EU jurisdiction. It also future-proofs the structure: as holons expand into different European markets, the SE can follow without the friction of cross-border corporate reorganization.

★ Bridge ★
Article IV — Function

Bridge to State Institutions

The published tagline — "Your Creation's Bridge to State Institutions" — captures the core function of Gruber Works. Creative and technological work does not naturally speak the language of state bureaucracy. Tax authorities require invoices with specific fields. Banks require articles of incorporation. Insurance providers require a legal person to underwrite. Gruber Works translates between these worlds.

What the Bridge Carries

Invoicing capacity and VAT compliance for any holon. Contractual standing for partnerships and client work. Banking and payment infrastructure under EU regulation. Liability containment that protects both the creator and the creation. Regulatory filings that keep the state satisfied without consuming the creator's attention.

The metaphor of a bridge is precise: Gruber Works does not absorb the holons into itself. Each project retains its own identity, culture, and operational logic. The SE simply provides the surface that state institutions can recognize and interact with. When a holon needs to sign a contract, issue an invoice, or open a bank account, it does so through Gruber Works — and the rest of the time, it operates freely under its own principles.

This is what #professionalizing_with_heart means in practice. The legal structure serves the creative mission rather than the other way around. Compliance is handled, but it does not define the work. The bridge exists so the creator can stay on the creative side of it.
