What is Soaro?

Soaro is the consulting and stewardship holon within the Bio pillar of EvoBioSys. It works with donor-recipient organisations and funders who understand that isolated efforts fail where coordinated efforts thrive.

Think of a Rubik’s cube: each face represents a stakeholder, each colour a resource or need. Random turns get nowhere. An algorithm — informed by metadata, real-time feedback, and the actual structure of the problem — solves it efficiently. That is what Soaro builds: a shared-outcome algorithm that maps interdependencies and reveals the moves that unlock the most value for everyone involved.

Core challenge: effectively allocating resources across complex, interdependent projects

The Framework

Soaro Five

Five pillars — like five petals of a flower — that move a collaboration from abstract goodwill to concrete, measurable shared success. Every Soaro engagement is structured around this framework.

Framework

Defined outcomes all partners commit to, with clear metrics and feedback loops that make progress visible to everyone.

Platform Modules

What the Soaro Workspace Does

The Soaro Stewardship workspace — in active development with Alia Tall-Khatib — is composed of interconnected, metadata-powered modules forming a coordination layer for multi-stakeholder initiatives.

Research

Structured research surfacing relevant data, precedent, and evidence to inform decisions across all partners.

Personalised Journey

Adaptive pathways that meet each participant where they are, guiding them through onboarding and contribution.

Local Partners

Anchoring global coordination in local knowledge, relationships, and on-the-ground trust.

Interdependent Opportunities

Illuminating hidden connections — where one organisation’s output is another’s input and collaboration becomes a multiplier.

Needs Identification

Systematic surfacing of unmet needs across the network, turning gaps into actionable items partners can address.

Metadata-Powered Synergy

Structured data about activities and intentions enabling algorithmic matching — the engine behind the shared-outcome algorithm.

Group Chat & Connections

Integrated communication and facilitated introductions that reduce friction between people who should be working together.

Tactical Support

Hands-on operational help — project management, capacity building, knowledge management, and resource libraries.

Measurement & Evaluation

Real-time feedback loops and structured M&E so every partner sees what is working and what to adjust next.

Soaro Ambassador Program

Ambassadors carry the Soaro vision into their own networks — connecting funders and donor-recipient organisations who believe collaboration matters. Each ambassador becomes a node in an expanding web of shared outcomes, amplifying Soaro’s reach with coordination at the speed the world needs.
