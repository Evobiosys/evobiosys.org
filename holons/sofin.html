What is SoFin?

SoFin — Social Finance — is the financial backbone of EvoBioSys. It holds the conviction that finance is not a neutral medium but a living force: it can regenerate communities or hollow them out, depending on how its flows are designed.

Where conventional finance optimizes for return on capital, SoFin optimizes for return on life. This means building financial instruments, tools, and practices that direct capital toward regenerative outcomes — community resilience, ecological restoration, and sovereign infrastructure — rather than toward extraction.

SoFin is a well-defined concept within the EvoBioSys ecosystem, carrying its own supertag and serving as both an operational domain and a philosophical anchor. It is the place where money meets meaning.

Governance

The SoFin Collective

The organizational body that stewards SoFin's practices, agreements, and projects.

The SoFin Collective is a nested holon — it sits under both the EvoBioSys Collective (as its financial sub-collective) and the broader SoFin concept (as the group that puts the philosophy into practice).

Current membership:

The Collective coordinates the practical work of SoFin — from managing the Funding Flow Machine to evaluating crypto projects, from building sovereign financial tools to establishing the protocols by which capital flows through the EvoBioSys network.

Funding Flow Machine

The Funding Flow Machine is SoFin's core infrastructure project — a system for tracking, directing, and visualizing how financial resources move through the EvoBioSys network and its allied projects.

Most organizations treat cash flow as a back-office concern. The Funding Flow Machine makes it a design surface. By making financial flows legible and intentional, it enables the SoFin Collective to:

  • Map inflows and outflows across all holons, making it visible where resources concentrate and where they are scarce.
  • Direct capital with intention — routing funds toward projects at critical growth stages rather than toward those with the loudest pitch.
  • Track regenerative impact — measuring not just financial return but the vitality of the systems that capital touches.

The Funding Flow Machine connects to SoFin's broader crypto and trading initiatives, including the SoFin bot (built on OctoBot), which explores automated and semi-automated trading strategies aligned with SoFin's regenerative principles.

Approach & principles

SoFin operates from a small set of commitments that distinguish it from conventional financial practice:

  • Sovereignty over dependency. Every financial tool SoFin builds should increase the autonomy of the people and communities that use it — never create lock-in.
  • Transparency by design. Financial flows should be legible to all participants. The Funding Flow Machine embodies this principle: money that moves through the network should be traceable, intentional, and accountable.
  • Regenerative returns. Capital is not neutral. SoFin evaluates every investment and flow by asking: does this regenerate living systems, or extract from them?
  • Crypto as tool, not ideology. Cryptocurrency and decentralized finance are valuable insofar as they serve sovereignty and regeneration. SoFin engages with crypto pragmatically, not dogmatically.
  • Collective stewardship. Financial decisions are made collectively through the SoFin Collective, not by individual mandate. As membership grows, so does the distributed wisdom of the system.
Money is a river. SoFin asks: where does it flow, what does it feed, and what does it leave behind?
