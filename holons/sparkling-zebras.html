What it is

What is Sparkling Zebras?

Sparkling Zebras is an in-person incubator experience that lives under idea2.Life. It is not a programme you apply to, not a pitch night, not an accelerator with a conveyor belt. It is a gathering — a space where vision meets capability and something real begins.

The name carries intent: zebras, not unicorns. Zebras are real, they move in herds, they are striking without pretending to be mythical. And the sparkling? That is the energy of the room when the right people sit around the right question with enough honesty to make something happen.

Sparkling Zebras operates as both a financial vehicle and an experiential incubator. It raises capital through its fractal-seed investment fund (connected to SoFin), and it deploys that capital not through term sheets alone, but through shared in-person time where stewards, builders, and supporters align around a living vision.

Tagged #my-legacy in the EvoBioSys system — this is one of Jakob’s core long-term commitments, a structure designed to outlast any single project or season.

The Experience

In-Person Incubator

That Spark is providing an in-person experience.

The core of Sparkling Zebras is not a document, a deck, or a Zoom call. It is a room. People gathered, in person, working on something that matters. The incubator experience is designed to compress time — to take what might otherwise drift through months of asynchronous deliberation and ground it in a few days of focused, embodied collaboration.

Think of it as alm gathering meets startup weekend, but without the performative urgency. The pace is intentional. The goal is not to ship a prototype by Sunday evening; it is to leave the room with enough shared understanding, enough alignment, and enough honest commitment that the work actually continues on Monday.

Each Sparkling Zebras experience begins with a vision brought by a steward — someone who has received an idea and cultivated it enough to invite others in. The experience then unfolds through stages designed to move that vision from concept to committed action.

Phase 01

The Gathering

Stewards, builders, and supporters arrive. The vision is shared in full — not as a pitch but as an invitation. The room listens, asks, and finds its shape.

Phase 02

The Alignment

Roles emerge. Resources are mapped. What needs to happen is separated from what would be nice. Honest conversations about capacity, risk, and commitment take place.

Phase 03

The Spark

Work begins. Not all of it — but the first real steps. Enough to prove the direction, build momentum, and create the bonds that will carry the project forward.

Phase 04

The Commitment

The experience closes with clear commitments: who does what, by when, with which resources. This is where intention becomes infrastructure.

Financial Vehicle

Fractal Seed Fund

Every incubator needs fuel. Sparkling Zebras houses a fractal seed investment fund — a financial vehicle connected to SoFin (Sovereign Finance) that raises and deploys capital at the earliest, most fragile stage of a project’s life.

The word “fractal” is deliberate. The fund replicates its own pattern: each investment seeds not only a project but the capacity for that project to eventually seed others. Capital flows outward in expanding circles, not upward through extraction.

The Vision

From Vision to Working Reality

The central purpose of Sparkling Zebras is to bridge the gap that kills most ideas: the distance between someone receiving a vision and people actually working on it. Most visions die in that gap — not because they lacked merit, but because they lacked the container to carry them from one mind into many hands.

Sparkling Zebras is that catalyst — the Spark in the middle. It takes the vision a steward has cultivated and creates the conditions for others to genuinely take ownership alongside them. Not as contractors, not as followers, but as co-builders who understand the vision well enough to carry it forward even when the steward is not in the room.

Going from the vision you receive by a steward to people actually working on it — that is what Sparkling Zebras exists to do.

This is why the in-person element is non-negotiable. Shared physical space creates a quality of understanding that no document, call, or message thread can replicate. When people have built something together in a room, they carry that shared experience forward as a foundation for everything that follows.

My Legacy

A Structure Built to Last

Sparkling Zebras is tagged #my-legacy because it is not a project with an end date. It is a pattern — a repeatable container for turning vision into reality. Each experience plants seeds that grow into new experiences. The incubator incubates itself. That is the fractal promise, and that is why it carries the weight of legacy.
