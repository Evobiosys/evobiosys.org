Investment · Animist · Living Systems

Investing for nature's values

EvoBioSys Capital represents the interests of living systems. We back ventures that eliminate the false distinction between humans and nature. We start with the question: what does nature value?

Our philosophy Investment focus

Philosophy

An animist worldview

In our animist worldview, the Earth is vividly alive and we are alive with it. We strive to see from the perspectives of ecosystems, species, and life processes — setting aside prevailing assumptions of human supremacy.

Two Time Horizons

Near term: "Nature" needs to become more legible to financial and technological systems. We advance definitions of measurement that include multiple ways of seeing and relating.

Long term: Free nature from the logic of human economics.

Measuring Life

Quantitative measurement combined with computation could further objectify and abstract nature. We seek to prevent this by advancing definitions that honor the aliveness and subtlety of living systems — not reduce them to price.

Leverage & Agency

Our highest leverage is to lend credence to daring undertakings that give nature visibility and agency. We back ventures whose work helps discover new ways of advocating for the diverse intelligences of the more-than-human world.

Biophilia Awakening

The human sensorium is thrillingly capable of establishing intimacy with the earth. We strive to awaken biophilia — the innate instinct to connect with life — so people arrive at a worldview of indivisibility and entanglement.

Investment Areas

Where we focus

Earth's Living Processes

Regenerating ecosystems and restoring what human intervention has diminished. We back initiatives that partner with nature to assist life's inherent circularity — redesigning linear systems, building biodiversity back into soils and landscapes, supporting indigenous knowledge, restoring wildlife connectivity.

Ownership & Decentralization

Communities own and actively create their local worlds. We search for tools and systems that enable self-organization, such that value is exalted and recirculated as the basis of a vital local economy.

Scientific Research

Science is demonstrating that nature is a living, relational process. We back research that exposes advanced communication and intelligence, with focus on new modes of sensing and measuring biodiversity.

Earth Jurisprudence

Legal systems define cultural norms and ethics. We empower approaches that advocate and litigate on behalf of the rights of all terrestrial beings and natural processes.

Policy & Regulation

Government is failing at protecting humans from ecologically harmful corporate actions. Aggressive policy entrepreneurship and smart new modes of organizing will correct regulatory capture and enable necessary innovation.

Activism & Journalism

Activists are crucial voices piercing systemic inertia, representing emergent worldviews and stories of environmental restoration. They are the 'voices of nature' speaking on behalf of subjugated ecosystems.

Origins

Ground Effect

The aerodynamic by which birds, insects and other flying creatures get more lift and less drag when they fly close to the ground.

We call ourselves EvoBioSys Capital because we gain buoyancy the closer we get to Earth.
