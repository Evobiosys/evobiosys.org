How We Partner

Three modes of collaboration

Inspired by how Jakob acts in the world: envisioning, curating, and setting up.

1

Envisioning & Aligning

Bridging worldviews. We help partners see the bigger picture, align on shared purpose, and design categorically-compatible visions that hold multiple perspectives without collapsing them.

2

Curating & Connecting

The EvoBioSys network is a living rolodex of aligned people and projects. We connect partners with the right collaborators, resources, and contexts to accelerate what matters.

3

Setting Up & Supporting

As a fractal co-founder, financial advisor, and strategic adviser, Jakob helps partners set up systems that begin with the infinite game in mind — stable structures designed for the long arc.
